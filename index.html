About Eric

Since 2011 I have worked as Director of Transportation at a local destination management company. In this role my major responsibilities centered around operations and management. During this time, however, I dabbled in a few side projects where I fumbled my way through building Wordpress sites. Despite the struggle, I loved the process of discovery and building something new. In 2018 I decided to delve deeper and began to teach myself, little by little, to program. However, this process, coupled with a full-time job, was slow and ambiguous. In order to expedite my learning I landed on DigitalCrafts to guide my way.

Along with programming a few of my interests include...

  • Music
  • Gaming
  • Spectator Sports
  • Cooking
  • Food(uh)
  • Film and Television
